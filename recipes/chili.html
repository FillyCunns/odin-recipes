Veggie Sweet Potato Instant Pot Chilli

This recipe is for a vegetarian chilli using Sweet Potatoes and Beans, cooked in an Instant Pot. It is ready in less than 30 minutes and is great for batch cooking.

Ingredients

The ingredients for the recipe are:

  • Sweet Potatoes (one medium)
  • Black Beans (one or two tins)
  • Tomatoes (one tin)
  • Stock (750ml)

Steps

  1. Chop potatoes into medium sized pieces
  2. All all ingredients to the pressure cooker
  3. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes
